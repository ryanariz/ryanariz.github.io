Anything-attempter and everything-admirer, Ryan Sky is a New Jersey-based multidisciplinary creative whose work spans branding, previsualization, immersive media, and web. Her work is shaped by the intense storytelling of Guy Ritchie's Snatch, the noir atmosphere of trip-hop's Massive Attack and Portishead, and the functional forms of Bauhaus design. Ryan has contributed design work for brands including Instagram, Meta, NYLFF, Spanx, Miller Lite, Marc Anthony, Celsius, YouTube, and CVS through collaborations with creative agencies. If you've got an idea, she'd probably like to try it — reach out here.
