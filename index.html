Take It To The Streets
In 2019, my two friends, Emily Peguero and Pablo Ledesma, hosted their first fashion show in North Bergen, NJ. They gave me the opportunity to create the promotional flyers for the event, which would be one of my first real attempts at graphic design. The original flyers are below.
While I was really proud of them back then, over time, as I improved as a designer and narrowed down my preferred styles, I realized they didn't show much of my personality, weren't demonstrative of my capabilities, and lacked structure and creativity overall. I decided to remake them, making sure to incorporate aspects of what I remember the show to be like, as well as a few of my own embellishments.

Door Study
You can tell a lot about a place by the door of its entrance. The likelihood of inner color abundance, how often passerbys come and go, or if they come at all. This door "study" started out with only home doors and the notion that doors are more the windows to a home than the windows themselves. The more door photos I took, though, the more my interest in doors in general grew.
If you happen to come across a flavorful door or entrance, and wish to share, feel free to send the address to my email located on my contact page. :)

Uncle Vinnies Comedy Club - Templates
Uncle Vinnies, once Ferrara's Ristorante, is currently a staple in Point Pleasant Beach, NJ. Having showcased professional comics such as Andrew DICE Clay, Jackie "The Joke Man" Martling, Vic Di Bitetto, Robert Kelly and more, the comedy club has been noted as one of the best by Night & Day Magazine, Stepping Out, NJ Monthly and Asbury Park Press.
As a diversifying business and client of the Syndicate, Uncle Vinnies requested many different kinds of master flyer templates to reuse for their varying events. I created the above two flyers as general templates for meet and greets and specil events hosted at Uncle Vinnies that were broader than just comic appearances.
Uncle Vinnies also collaborates with businesses such as Max's Es-ca, the Algonquin Arts Theatre, State Theater and American Hotel. Above, is a template flyer for events hosted by Uncle Vinnies but situated in one of the aformentioned locations. The club also wanted to find a way to present, in one flyer, multiple comics that would perform during the week, at both Uncle Vinnies and other locations. Below, are two iterations of that request.

CraftMix Rebrand + Package Design
For this conceptual rebrand, I decided to take on my first package design project, which included a mix of 2D dieline design and 3D animation.
